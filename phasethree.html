From 2000 to present, we have been able to
take massive leaps of progress in our knowledge
of artificial intelligence. Similar to Deep Blue defeating
Kasparov, there were many landmark milestones in artificial
intelligence. For example, the first humanoid robot Sophia
was introduced in 2016, and in 2017, AlphaZero, a AI developed by
Deepmind at Google crushed Stockfish a chess engine at a game of
chess.
