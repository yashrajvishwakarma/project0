What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence is a branch of computer science
which deals with giving inanimate objects human intelligence, and
essentially allow it to think and behave like a human. Learn more about
what artificial intelligence is here!

The concept of artifical intelligence was laid down by Alan Turing in 1950,
in his paper, "Computing Machinery and Intelligence", which would inspire the 1956 Dartmouth
Conference in New Hampshire. Learn more about artificial intelligence here!
