The concept of Artificial Intelligence began with the great Alan Turing, whose invention of the
first computer would inspire him to write his paper, "Computing Machinery and Intelligence" in 1950, which would lead
to the creation of the term "Artificial Intelligence" for the first time in 1956 at the Dartmouth College Conference.
